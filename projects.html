For my Action project for Honors English this year, my group and I decided to go into Hoover Elementary school in Oakland and help inspire younger students to see the opputunities that exist in the world. We first built marshmellow and pasta towers, to encourage their interest in STEM. The students loved this activity and many said that it helped them to realize that they want to be engineers in the future. On the second day visiting the school, we drew with the kids, helping them see the artisitc side of the world. This was an acomplishment for me because I built really strong relationships with intelligant and kind younger students and helped them to see the amazing future they can have.
